Date: Sun, 9 Feb 1992 22:58 EST
From: BLOODY GREAT DRUMKIT 
Subject: Oh man where have I been.....

	Evening all, though I should be doing my work, I decided to catch up
on the NMS.  I also learned that one does not unsubscribe from the NMS over a
college break, because you never have any idea what will be going on when you
come back.

	I love everyone's T-shirt ideas, has anyone suggested "Rush-mgr:
Working Man does Marathon Mission not for Big Money, but of Freewill? You Bet
Your Life!" Cheezy, but accurate.  Kudos to Dave our Rush-mgr!! :)

	Also, last friday Subdivisions, a Rush cover band, played here at
Clark.  They weren't half bad (not the real McCoy, but got my fix), they
played the spectrum, from Working Man to Where's My Thing?, musically I gave
them an A-/B+, but the kid sure wasn't Geddy singing though.  Frank's little
review column recommends the show.

	Til next time, farewell where ever you fare...

	Frank.

here. As I said to James ?, there's a lot of burocracy. And... I don't know

or not.
