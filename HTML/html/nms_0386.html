From: becker@areaplg2.corp.mot.com (John Becker)
Date: Mon, 25 Nov 91 11:37:08 CST
Subject: Catching RTB on MTV...don't delay!

It is obvious that MTV is running the RTB video in what they call
"light rotation". If the video gets a lot of good response, they MIGHT
shift it to heavy rotation, but from past experiences with Rush vids on
MTV, it is far more likely that they will drop it entirely after a few
weeks. Since it has been on for about 3 weeks now. I strongly suggest that
anyone who is interested in catching it on tape get very serious about it
ASAP. I probably taped 30 hours of MTV before I got it (That was in the
first week, and it may not have actually been on the first few days.)

Buy a TV Guide and look at their schedule, so you don't waste taping time
on all their BS segments. Tape 6 hours of vids, scan through it, and if
you didn't get it try again! I was able to tape 18 hours of MTV in one 24
hour period without getting up in the middle of the night and without
taking off from work. If they are still running it, you should get it in
less than 2 days with this technique, and with a little luck you'll catch
it in the first or second taping session. Don't wait or it'll be gone.

John
